Fish and chips


Ingredients

  • 1 x Fish
  • 1 x Portion of chips
  • Salt, pepper and a good splash of vinegar to taste
  • Mush peas optional






Method

For best results select one of the following options:

  1. Phone for a takeaway from a proper fish and chip shop or (b);
  2. Ask someone who knows what they're doing to cook for you

Please note regardless of which method you choose, a serving a cold beer strongly recommended
